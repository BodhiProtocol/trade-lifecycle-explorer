# Trade Lifecycle Explorer

**An interactive map of capital markets — six pillars, one lifecycle.**

Live demo → https://bodhiprotocol.github.io/trade-lifecycle-explorer/

---

## Why this exists

The trade lifecycle is usually taught as a wall of text. This is the opposite: a lotus mandala of the **six pillars** that hold up every capital market, and an interactive walk through one equity trade — from order initiation to settled, reconciled record.

Built by a capital markets business analyst on one belief:

> *Understanding is downstream of making.*

## The six pillars

| # | Pillar | One line |
|---|--------|----------|
| I | Market Structure & Participants | A market is a conversation. Know who is speaking. |
| II | Financial Instruments | Everything traded is a promise, shaped differently. |
| III | Trade Lifecycle | Born, matched, cleared, settled, remembered. |
| IV | Clearing & Settlement | Trust, made mechanical. |
| V | Risk & Compliance | The market's conscience, encoded. |
| VI | Technology & Data | The invisible loom on which every trade is woven. |

Each pillar carries a **BA Lens** — what a business analyst actually does at that layer.

## The lifecycle walk

Six stages of an Indian equity trade on the T+1 cycle:

`Order Initiation → Execution → Capture & Enrichment → Clearing → Settlement → Post-Settlement`

Each stage shows who acts, the key rule, the risk, and the BA's job there.

## Tech

One self-contained `index.html`. No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies. Vanilla SVG + JS. Open the file, it works.
